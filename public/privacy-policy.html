Privacy Policy

ODU Yoruba Writer  |  Effective date: 24 February 2026  |  App ID: com.yoruba.odu

ODU Yoruba Writer ("the App") is developed and maintained by Peace Toba Olatunji (Dopay). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

Short version: The App collects only the information you voluntarily provide (email, username, location). It contains no ads, no third-party tracking beyond Firebase (Google), and does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, or GPS location.

1. Information We Collect

Account information (optional) — If you create an account:

  • Email address — used for login via Firebase Authentication.
  • Username — a display name you choose; shown on the leaderboard.
  • Self-reported location — a city or country you type in; never obtained from GPS.
  • Account creation timestamp.

Contributions you submit (optional) — If you submit Yoruba/English sentence pairs through the App:

  • The English and Yoruba text you enter.
  • Your username and self-reported location (if provided).
  • Submission timestamp and challenge category.

Contributions are stored locally on your device and may be synced to our systems to support language-training datasets. You can export or delete your local contributions at any time from within the App.

Leaderboard data — Usernames, contribution counts, and locations of top contributors are listed publicly in the leaderboard. This data is sourced from a Google Sheet that is publicly accessible.

Usage analytics (automatic) — Firebase Analytics may collect anonymous event data (e.g. screens viewed, session duration) to help us improve the App. No personally identifying information is linked to analytics events.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your account and keep your session secure.
  • To display your username and location on the leaderboard and contributor map.
  • To build and improve Yoruba language datasets for open research.
  • To detect and address abuse or policy violations (admin moderation).
  • To understand how the App is used so we can make it better.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use it for advertising.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Firebase (Google LLC) — Authentication, cloud database (Firestore), and analytics. Google may process data on servers in the United States and other countries. See Firebase Privacy and Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Sheets (Google LLC) — The leaderboard is read from a publicly accessible Google Sheet CSV. No user data is written to Google Sheets by the App.

The App does not integrate any advertising networks, third-party analytics SDKs (beyond Firebase), social login providers, or payment processors.

4. Permissions Requested

On Android, the App requests only one permission:

  • INTERNET — Required to connect to Firebase and fetch leaderboard data.

The App does not request access to your GPS location, camera, microphone, contacts, storage, or any other sensitive system resources.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Account profiles and admin flags are stored in Firebase Firestore, hosted by Google.
  • Contributions are cached locally in your device's browser storage (localStorage) until synced.
  • Leaderboard data is cached locally to reduce network requests.
  • All communication with Firebase uses HTTPS/TLS encryption in transit.
  • We apply reasonable technical measures to protect stored data, but no system is completely secure.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

You may delete your account and all associated personal data at any time using our dedicated deletion page:

→ Request account and data deletion

Upon verified request, we will delete your account record from Firebase within 30 days and send a confirmation. Locally cached data on your device is removed when you uninstall the App.

Contribution text that has already been incorporated into published language datasets may be retained in anonymised form for research integrity.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is designed as an educational and cultural language tool suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us and we will promptly delete the account and associated data.

If you are a parent or guardian and you become aware that your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us at the address below.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your country or region, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data — see Delete My Account.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities.
  • Data portability (export your contributions via the in-app CSV export).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address in Section 9.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, please open an issue on our public repository:

  • GitHub: github.com/Dopayarts/Odu/issues
  • Account deletion: data-deletion.html

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
