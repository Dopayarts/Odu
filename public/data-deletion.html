Delete My Account

ODU Yoruba Writer  |  com.yoruba.odu

You can request the deletion of your ODU account and all associated personal data at any time. There is no waiting period — we will process your request within 30 days of verification.

This action is permanent. Deleted accounts and personal data cannot be recovered.

What gets deleted

  • Your Firebase Authentication account (email address, login credentials)
  • Your user profile in Firestore (username, location, account metadata)
  • Any admin flags or warnings associated with your account

What is retained

  • Yoruba/English contribution text that has already been incorporated into published language datasets may be retained in anonymised form for research integrity — your username and email are removed from these records.
  • Local data stored on your own device (contributions cached in browser storage) is not held by us — uninstalling the app removes it from your device.

How to request deletion

Click the button below to open a pre-filled deletion request on GitHub. You must include the email address registered to your account so we can verify and locate it.

Submit Deletion Request on GitHub

You will need a free GitHub account to submit the form.
The issue will be kept private by the maintainer and closed once deletion is confirmed.

No GitHub account?

If you do not have a GitHub account, you can request deletion by opening a new issue manually at github.com/Dopayarts/Odu/issues with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and your registered email address.

Expected timeline

  • Acknowledgement within 3 business days
  • Full deletion within 30 days of identity verification
  • Confirmation reply once deletion is complete
